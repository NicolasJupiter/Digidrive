About

This is a place for zen, peace, and meditation. Ok seriously, this is a virtual distributed HDD where generous users such as yourself share content (such as videos) already stored in your pocket cloud storage such as Google drive. This is a simplistic version and based on feedback, demand, willing partners, etc, we could turn this into something really awesome. If you are interested, do please contact me using the contact form.


Steps to submit a video:

Firstly, the video needs to be already uploaded in your google drive. So, do that first, if not done already. Then, in your drive, right click and go to 'preview'. The video should pop-up and load. In the top right, hit the three dots (menu) and click share. In the 'share' dialogue box, press 'get sharable link' [IMPORTANT: Ensure that anyone with the link can view the video]. Now copy that link and paste it in your browser and go there. The same video would load but it would now cover the whole page. Click on the three dots in the top right. Then click on embed item. A dialogue box would open up an iframe tag. Copy the contents/ value of the src, without quotes (removing the quotes is very important, if not done currectly, it would cause hurricanes all over the world) and paste it in our 'Submit video' form in the Video path field. Enter the other details and hit submit. The video would be submitted and the page would reload.

Dont panic if the page reloads onto the submit form with your data still there. The video has been successfully submited and is available in the queue for playing. Simply close the form, return to the landing page and play your next video from your virtual HDD
